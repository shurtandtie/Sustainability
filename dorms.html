What do carbon emissions look like in our day-to-day?



Let's take a look at the top contributors to building emissions at Northeastern in 2022.










The buildings that are leading contributors to our footprint are Egan Research Center, Stetson East, Hurtig Hall, Marino Recreation Center, and Mugar Life Sciences Building. These are all high-traffic buildings central to student life. More than half of these are buildings that house science labs engaging in energy-demanding research.



















Strategically decreasing energy usage in these areas is critical to meet our BERDO goals.
