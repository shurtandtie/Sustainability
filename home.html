How is Northeastern University building a sustainable tomorrow?

Our Green Mission

Welcome to Northeastern University! Our bustling campus is home to several large-scale services and resources, but our sustainability efforts are always front of mind.


From the dining halls to the academic buildings, Northeastern is committed to ensuring a robust system that considers sustainability and maintenance. With transparency as a key priority, we have made accessible data that tells you how some of the buildings you frequent during your day are part of our sustainable ecosystem.
